Instructions:


• In a large bowl, mix together ground meat, shredded carrots, shredded cabbage, minced onions, minced garlic, soy sauce,

cornstarch, salt, and pepper.

• Place a wrapper on a flat surface with one corner facing you.

• Place 2 tablespoons of the meat mixture on the wrapper.

• Roll the wrapper tightly around the filling, tucking in the sides as you go.

• Repeat steps 2-4 with the remaining wrappers and filling.

• Heat oil in a pan over medium-high heat.

• Once the oil is hot, add the lumpia and fry until golden brown.

• Once cooked, remove from oil and drain on a paper towel.

• Serve with sweet chili sauce, if desired.


Enjoy your homemade lumpia!
